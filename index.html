Don't Breath!

Hold a key down on your keyboard to start the timer while you take a deep breath in. Release the key when you can no longer hold your breath. The timer will tell you how long you held it!

Disclaimer: Holding your breath for extended periods can be dangerous and is not recommended. It may result in oxygen deprivation, which can have adverse effects on your health, including dizziness, cognitive impairment, and, in extreme cases, loss of consciousness. Please exercise caution and avoid engaging in prolonged breath-holding activities.
